Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about secure SMTP automation.

How does Mass Email PRO stay 100% free?
Mass Email PRO is an independent developer tool built to eliminate recurring software costs for founders, marketers, and students. It is monetized strictly via optional community coffee contributions.
How do I generate an App Password for Gmail?
Google requires 2-Step Verification to send automated SMTP emails. Go to your Google Account Security settings, enable 2-Step Verification, search for "App Passwords", and generate a 16-character password to paste into Step 1.
Are my contact CSVs and email addresses secure?
Yes. Unlike traditional web applications that upload files to backend servers, Mass Email PRO parses contact spreadsheets directly inside your web browser's RAM. No recipient data is ever transmitted to or stored on our servers.
How do I prevent my emails from landing in spam folders?
To ensure maximum inbox placement, use a verified domain with active SPF and DKIM DNS records. Additionally, configure a 3 to 5 second send delay in Step 4 to avoid triggering ISP velocity filters.
